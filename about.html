About the organisation

The story behind WECAN

WECAN Events & Promotions (Pty) Ltd is a family-owned event planning and talent promotion company founded on July 7th, 2025 to turn event spaces into real platforms for opportunity.

Organisation overview

Created to solve a real gap

WECAN was built around a simple idea: great events should do more than entertain. They should also make room for rising talent to be seen, trusted, and booked professionally.

The company grew from real experience in hosting events and seeing how difficult it can be for clients to connect with fresh performers and for talented young creatives to get structured exposure.

Current position

From social media reliance to a professional platform

Before this website, WECAN depended mostly on Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and word of mouth. This website now gives the business a central place to present services, showcase work, and manage both bookings and talent submissions more professionally.
