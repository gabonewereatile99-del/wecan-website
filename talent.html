Talent discovery platform

Submit your profile and let your work speak loudly

The Talent Hub gives young creatives a more professional route into real bookings through a structured profile submission instead of scattered social media direct messages.

Who should apply?

DJs, MCs, dancers, spoken word artists, vocalists, hosts, and other performers who want event opportunities.

What to prepare

A short professional bio, your city, a performance link, your social handles, and an optional media kit or profile image.

Why this page matters

It helps WECAN review talent consistently and match the right creatives to the right audiences.

Simple two-step UX

Designed to reduce submission drop-off
