Frequently asked questions

Helpful answers for clients and performers

Clients complete the enquiry form, choose an event package, and share the date, guest count, location, and goals. The website then gives a quick budget estimate and WECAN follows up with a tailored consultation.

The Talent Hub is designed specifically for creatives. It captures a performance category, short bio, city, social links, and media so the team can review submissions professionally.

The enquiry page focuses on event bookings and pricing. The contact page is for general messages, partnerships, press, feedback, and other communication that is not a booking request.
